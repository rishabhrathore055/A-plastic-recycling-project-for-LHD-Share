What are the benefits of reycling and how will it help me? Recycling reduces the use of natural resources by reusing materials. This overall helps our environemnt by making it more sustainable and healthy.

How and where should I start? You can start recycling by simply joining your local recycling programm and by knowing what to put in the Recycling Bin. Try to keep it simple in the beginning and focus on the most commonly recycled items like paper, glass, aluminum and plastic.

What exactly is recycling? Recycling is the process of collecting and processing materials that would otherwise be thrown away as trash and turning them into new products. Recycling can benefit your community and the environment.

How can I reuse the things I already have? Recycling is very important, but even better is reusing. Recycling actually uses energy, transport time, and fuel to get the items to where they need to go. If you can reuse an item yourself, that's more energy, time, and fuel saved, as well as less emissions produced. Go and check out the ideas sections for ideas on how you can reuse the recyclable items at your home.
